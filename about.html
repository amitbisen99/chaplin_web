Meet Your Chaplain

Sanjay Mathur

A Hindu Chaplain and community leader dedicated to fostering spiritual care, interfaith understanding, and compassionate service.

Background & Training

Sanjay Mathur is a Clinical Pastoral Education (CPE)-trained chaplain who has completed four units of CPE in hospital settings at UR Medicine and Rochester Regional Health. His extensive training combines the depth of Hindu spiritual wisdom with modern counseling techniques to provide holistic care.

  • Clinical Pastoral Education (CPE) Certified
  • Four units of CPE in hospital settings
  • Training at UR Medicine
  • Training at Rochester Regional Health
  • Interfaith dialogue and understanding expertise

Approach to Spiritual Care

Drawing upon the rich traditions of Hindu philosophy—including the wisdom of dharma, karma, and seva—Sanjay provides compassionate guidance that honors individual spiritual journeys while addressing practical life challenges. His approach integrates active listening, cultural sensitivity, and deep respect for each person's unique path.

Our Mission

Bridging Tradition and Modern Care

To provide culturally sensitive, spiritually grounded support that helps Hindu individuals and families navigate life's challenges with wisdom, compassion, and connection to their faith traditions—whether in hospitals, universities, or community settings.

Guiding Principles

Core Values

Seva (Service)

Selfless service forms the foundation of chaplaincy work. We serve without expectation, offering support to all who need spiritual guidance and compassionate care.

Karuna (Compassion)

Deep empathy and compassion guide every interaction. We meet individuals where they are, honoring their experiences and supporting their healing journey.

Dharma (Righteous Living)

We help individuals connect with their dharma—their purpose and path—providing guidance that aligns with Hindu wisdom while respecting each person's unique journey.
